    Family

    Parents

    My parents are amazing. They did everything they could for me and, even when I didn't realize it, made many sacrifices for my well-being and future. Both of my parents are supportive of my career and have always validated the accomplishments I've been able to achieve. My mom, a very timid and cautious woman, doesn't let her emotions get in the way of doing whatever it takes to care for her sons. She puts her full effort into understanding us. My dad made many sacrifices to provide the resources his sons needed for a better life than he had. He also has a great sense of humor and knows how to lighten and brighten any tough situation. I love them both and am truly grateful to have grown up with such supportive parents.

    Brothers

    I have three older brothers—David, Vick, and Danny (from left to right). My brothers have been a massive support not just in my plans for college or my future career, but throughout my entire life, no matter the situation I've faced. Even though we've had some issues in the past and didn't always have the strongest bond, as I grow older and begin to face the real world, I'm grateful to have them supporting me every step of the way into adulthood. Their tough love and deeply earned affection have helped me mature and take life more seriously. I'm forever grateful that they are my brothers.
